Pancakes

Description

Recipe for yummy pancakes.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together.
  2. Make a well, then add the wet ingredients. Stir to combine.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle och pan.
  4. Cook for two to three minutes, then flip.
  5. Continue cooking until brow on both sides.
