Scones

Description

Simple scones.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients, then work the butter into the dough using two knives or a pastry blender.
  2. Whisk the milk and egg togerther, then stir the egg mixture into the flour mixture.
  3. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead briefly.
  4. Pat or roll the dough into a round and cut into triangles using a knife or a bench scraper.
  5. Bake the scones in a preheated oven until golden brown
